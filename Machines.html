Espresso Machine

Brewing Method Used: Uses high pressure to pass hot water through finely ground coffee in a short time, producing a concentrated and rich espresso.


Pressure Level: The pump pressure typically ranges between 9-15 bars.


Coffee Grind Size Suitable for Device: Fine grind.


Type of Coffee Beans Used: Suitable for all types of coffee beans, but darker roasts like Robusta beans or dark blends are often preferred.


French Press

Brewing Method Used: Coffee grounds are steeped in hot water for a set time, then separated by pressing down to keep the coffee grounds below the filter.


Pressure Level: No mechanical pressure; relies only on immersion.


Coffee Grind Size Suitable for Device: Coarse grind.


Type of Coffee Beans Used: Best for mild or fruity coffee, like Arabica beans with a medium roast.


Electric Drip Coffee Maker

Brewing Method Used: The device heats water to an optimal temperature, which then flows over ground coffee slowly, dripping into a glass or ceramic carafe ready for serving.


Pressure Level: Relies on gravity for water flow, without any added pressure.


Coffee Grind Size Suitable for Device: Medium grind.


Type of Coffee Beans Used: Best suited for light to medium roast coffee, like Arabica beans, to provide a smooth and balanced flavor.


Cold Brew Tower

Brewing Method Used: Utilizes a slow drip process where cold water seeps through coarsely ground coffee over an extended period (usually 12-24 hours), producing a smooth, concentrated coffee concentrate.


Pressure Level: Relies on gravity and time, with no mechanical pressure involved in the brewing process.


Coffee Grind Size Suitable for Device: Coarse grind.


Type of Coffee Beans Used: Typically made with medium or dark roast beans, such as Arabica or a blend, to enhance the rich flavors without acidity.


Nitro Cold Brew Maker

Brewing Method Used: Starts with cold brew coffee concentrate and infuses it with nitrogen gas, resulting in a creamy, frothy texture and a smooth mouthfeel, often served straight from a tap.


Pressure Level: Uses nitrogen pressure, typically around 30-40 PSI, to infuse the cold brew coffee.


Coffee Grind Size Suitable for Device: Coarse grind.


Type of Coffee Beans Used: Works well with various coffee beans, but many prefer using a medium to dark roast for a richer flavor profile that balances well with the nitrogen infusion.
